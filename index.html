Welcome to the Library

A Library Management System (LMS) helps libraries manage and organize their resources more efficiently.
Book Tracking: It keeps track of all the books in the library, including available and borrowed ones.
Issuing and Returning Books: Helps manage the process of issuing books to students or users and tracks their return dates.
Catalog Search: Users can easily search for books by title, author, or subject.
User Management: Keeps records of library users, such as students or members, including their borrowing history.
Alerts: Sends reminders to users about overdue books or upcoming return dates.
Management: Helps librarians maintain a catalog of new arrivals and remove old or damaged books.
